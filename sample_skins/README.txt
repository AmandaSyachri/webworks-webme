All designs in this directory are either GPL or Creative Commons designs that have been adapted to work in the Webworks-WebME engine.

To use any of these designs, simply copy the directory into your ww.skins directory. For example, copy "theisland" to "/ww.skins/theisland". Then in your admin area, go to Site Options (under Users And Admins) and choose the template to make it the default.
